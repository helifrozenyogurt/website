How it works

Simple for the property. Fun for everyone else.

Heli is designed to make placement feel straightforward from the first conversation to launch day.

  • We review the location, visibility, power access, service access, and foot traffic fit.
  • We coordinate machine placement, launch support, and optional branded visuals for the location.
  • Guests use the machine directly, with cashless payment and simple instructions.
  • Heli manages the ongoing service plan, product refills, and operational support.
  • Your venue earns a revenue share from every cup sold, with transparent monthly reporting.
